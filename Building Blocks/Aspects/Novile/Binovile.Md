# Binovile

## Overview
The binovile, or 80° aspect, doubles the novile and highlights stages of integration that require partnership, feedback, and collaborative refinement. It invites people to share their gifts in supportive networks.

## Key Traits
- Encourages co-creation and reciprocal mentorship.
- Shows where refinement occurs through iterative cycles of teaching and learning.
- Points to opportunities for community building rooted in compassion and mutual respect.

## Example Interpretations
1. **Natal Chart Example**
   In a natal chart, a binovile emphasizes co-creative integration. The individual grows by exchanging feedback, teaching what they know, and inviting trusted partners to help refine emerging talents.
2. **Transit Example**
   Transit binoviles open doors to collaborative refinement. Accepting invitations to co-host events, join study groups, or participate in mutual aid allows shared wisdom to flourish.
3. **Synastry Example**
   Between charts, a binovile thrives on reciprocal mentorship. The relationship deepens through shared practice spaces, rotating leadership, and compassionate accountability that elevate everyone involved.
4. **Progressed Chart Example**
   A progressed binovile signals a period of integrating mastery through community. The native can coordinate collective projects, design peer learning circles, or formalize partnerships that harness combined strengths.

## Use In Practice
Seek feedback loops and shared practice spaces. The binovile strengthens when gifts are exchanged and refined within compassionate communities.
