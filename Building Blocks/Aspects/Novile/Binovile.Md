# Binovile

## Overview
The binovile, or 80° aspect, doubles the novile and highlights stages of integration that require partnership, feedback, and collaborative refinement. It invites people to share their gifts in supportive networks.

## Key Traits
- Encourages co-creation and reciprocal mentorship.
- Shows where refinement occurs through iterative cycles of teaching and learning.
- Points to opportunities for community building rooted in compassion and mutual respect.

## Examples
- Binoviles appear in charts of facilitators, ensemble leaders, or cooperative founders who thrive on collaborative growth.
**Natal Chart Example.** Someone with Mercury binovile Venus might become a beloved workshop host, blending storytelling and art therapy in circles that help participants embrace healing with grace.
**Transit Example.** When transiting Sun binoviles natal Jupiter, invitations to collaborate surface—co-authoring a course, co-leading a retreat, or launching a charitable initiative that amplifies shared wisdom.
**Synastry Example.** If one partner’s Saturn binoviles the other’s Moon, their commitment may revolve around building safe containers—perhaps founding a community center or support network that nurtures emotional resilience.

## Use In Practice
Seek feedback loops and shared practice spaces. The binovile strengthens when gifts are exchanged and refined within compassionate communities.
